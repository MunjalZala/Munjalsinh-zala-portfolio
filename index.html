About

I'm Munjalsinh Zala, a Mechatronics Engineering student at SRH University Berlin (GPA 1.9 German Scale), with a hands-on focus on structural design, FEM simulation, and aerospace-grade testing.

Currently serving as Mechanical Design Lead on the CanSat Competition 2026 — a NASA/NRL-sponsored rocket payload mission. I design for real constraints: 30G ejection shock, tight mass budgets, and GPS-guided paraglider descent.

I also contribute as a Rover Mechanical Engineer for the European Rover Challenge 2026, helping improve stability in a six-wheel rocker-bogie system with four points of contact while the team moves through Preliminary Design Review.

My certifications in SolidWorks and AutoCAD back up practical experience from an engineering internship and metalworking training at Handwerkskammer Potsdam. I'm actively seeking an internship to apply structural design and validation skills at the cutting edge of sUAS / eVTOL development.
